Brooklyn Museum of Art

A personal collection of some of my Favorite Art from the Brooklyn Museum.

About

I haven't involved myself to much into the art world so this site has been a means for me to further expand my exposure to art from different regions of the world. I've compiled a gallery from four regions (Europe, American, Asian, Islamic) all sourced from the Brooklyn Museum of Art. Each gallery consist of art that I found visually fascinating, which to my surprise seem to fall into the categories of grim and ghoulish to bleak and grim. Leave a comment below to let me know what you think.
